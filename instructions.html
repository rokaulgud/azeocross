Instructions

Solving the Crossword

  • Click a cell on the crossword grid, or click a clue to enter your guess in the grid.
  • Click twice on a cell to toggle between across and down.
  • The active cell is highlighted in blue
  • Start typing your guess and hit enter when you are done typing to move on to the next word.
  • The word will turn green or red if you get it right or wrong.
  • You can use the tab and shift-tab keys and the arrow keys, to move around the crossword.
